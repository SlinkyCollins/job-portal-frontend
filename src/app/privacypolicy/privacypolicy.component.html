Privacy Policy for JobNet

JobNet respects your privacy. When you sign in using Google or Facebook, we collect your name, email, and profile picture only for account creation and login purposes. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

Your data is securely stored and used solely to enhance your experience on JobNet. We take all reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Data Deletion

You can delete your JobNet account and all associated data at any time by going to your Account Settings and selecting the Delete Account option. If you need assistance, you may also send a request to: jobnetsupport@new.com.

Once deleted, your account and all stored data will be permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.
